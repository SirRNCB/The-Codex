"A server dedicated to my community and the fans who read my works and support me."

The Hall of Ink is the official Discord community established by Sir R.N.C. It serves as the central communications hub for followers of the author's work, providing a direct line of contact between the creator and the audience.

Structure & Organization

The server is organized into specific categories designed to facilitate different types of interaction:

  • The Scriptorium: The primary social space for general conversation and community bonding.
  • The Archives: A dedicated workspace for writers to share works-in-progress (WIPs), request feedback, and access writing resources.
  • The Trials: An interactive zone featuring writing sprints, prompts, and community challenges.
  • Echoes: Voice channels for live discussions, readings, and study sessions.

Purpose

Established in January 2026, the primary goal of The Hall of Ink is to foster a supportive environment for both consumers and creators of fiction. It allows readers to receive real-time notifications regarding Project: Visual Novel and The Silent Verse.
