The Hall of Ink

The Hall of Ink
Type Pocket Dimension
Ruler Sir R.N.C
Status Active
Affiliation The High Council

"You are all my servants from now on. Whether you want to serve me alive or dead is immaterial to me."

The Hall of Ink is a metaphysical space that exists between the creative mind and the output. It is said to be the place where characters go when they are forgotten by their authors, waiting to be written back into existence.

Description

The Hall appears as an infinite library, but the books are blank. The only light comes from the Inkwell, a massive pool of black liquid in the center of the atrium. The architecture shifts depending on the genre currently being writtenâ€”becoming gothic stone during horror arcs, or chrome steel during sci-fi drafts.

History

First discovered during the Great Writer's Block of 2024, it has since served as the primary base of operations. It is rumored that the Hall connects all of the author's distinct universes, acting as a crossroads where characters from Project: Visual Novel might catch glimpses of entities from The Silent Verse.
