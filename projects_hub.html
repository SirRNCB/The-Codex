THE MANIFESTO

"The blueprint of worlds yet to come."

Here lies the categorization of all intellectual properties managed by Sir R.N.C. Projects are divided into specific formats.

Web Light Novels

The Format: Short-form narratives characterized by rapid pacing, heavy dialogue, and episodic releases optimized for digital consumption.

The Objective: While rooted in the aesthetics of Japanese media, my focus here is on the accessibility of the format. I utilize this medium to deploy stories that are fast, agile, and free from the structural density of traditional literature.

Web Novels

The Format: Long-form fiction published serially on the open web. Unlike static publishing, these stories evolve chapter by chapter, allowing for sprawling plots that react to the live feedback of the reader.

The Objective: This is the primary engine of the archive. I use this format to construct expansive sagas that grow over time, allowing for a depth of world-building that shorter formats cannot sustain.

Interactive Novels

The Format: Text-driven experiences where the reader assumes the role of the protagonist. These narratives hinge on agency, offering branching paths and divergent endings based on the choices made throughout the story.

The Objective: To breach the wall between author and observer. These projects are experiments in agency, testing how different choices can fracture a single timeline into multiple distinct realities.

Fan Fictions

The Format: Transformative works anchored in pre-existing intellectual properties. These projects explore divergent timelines, "what-if" scenarios, and character studies within established multiverses.

The Objective: A tribute to the architects who came before. This archive allows me to step into universes I admire but did not build, expanding on their lore and exploring the corners of worlds that their original creators left in shadow.

Books

The Format: Traditional, self-contained literary constructs. Unlike serials, these works are drafted as complete manuscripts, focusing on structural integrity, polished prose, and a definitive beginning, middle, and end.

The Objective: The pursuit of permanence. While the web is fluid, a book is static and final. My ultimate ambition is to forge a manuscript capable of standing within the physical world, whether through self-publishing or traditional avenues.

Visual Novels

The Format: A multimedia convergence of literature, art, and sound. These projects utilize character sprites, backgrounds, and audio to create an immersive, atmospheric reading experience.

The Objective: The ultimate convergence. The long-term protocol is to adapt the text-only archives (Web Novels) into this immersive format. Resources permitting, this serves as the final, definitive version of the stories told here.
